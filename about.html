ABOUT CRITICONN-2025

ABOUT CRITICONN

The Faculty of Nursing at Parul University proudly inaugurated the National Critical Care Conference - CRITICONN in 2022. This initiative was launched with the vision to explore the ever-evolving domain of critical care nursing, bringing together nursing professionals, educators, and students from across the nation. CRITICONN serves as an exceptional platform designed to enhance the critical care skills of nursing practitioners and provide unparalleled opportunities for academic development and professional growth. In a world where critical care nursing is vital to saving lives and ensuring the best patient outcomes, the conference aims to address the current trends, challenges, and advancements within this specialized field. CRITICONN is a unique opportunity for nursing students to engage with experts, gain exposure to cutting-edge knowledge, and develop the necessary skills to excel in critical care environments. The conference also fosters collaboration among healthcare professionals, enabling the exchange of ideas, experiences, and best practices to further elevate the standards of care. Through workshops, lectures, panel discussions, and interactive sessions, CRITICONN strives to provide a comprehensive learning experience. The conference empowers nursing students to become confident, skilled, and compassionate healthcare providers, equipped to meet the demands of critical care nursing with expertise and commitment. The ultimate goal of CRITICONN is to nurture the next generation of critical care nurses who can contribute significantly to the advancement of healthcare in our communities, ensuring better patient outcomes and promoting a culture of excellence in critical care nursing.
